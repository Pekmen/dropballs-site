What is this???

This, my friend, is a Dropbox based chat built entirely from sphagetti code and love. Although barely working, we have been using it as an exclusive mean of comunication for a couple of years now.

Feature

Tired of encrypted messages and boring bug free chat? This app uses only shared nonencrypted plaintext as a chat log.

Feature

Make yourself part of your machine! With Dropballs you have to manualy type "/r" when you want to see incoming message.

Feature

Support of up to five (5) colors for nicks to easily manage your chatting. Didn't saw that anywhere, right?
