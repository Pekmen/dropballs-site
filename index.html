What is this???

This, my friend, is a Dropbox based chat built entirely from sphagetty code and love. Although barely working, we have been using it as an exclusive mean of comunication for a couple of years now.

Feature

Tired of encrypted messages and boring bug free chat? This app using only shared nonencrypted plaintext as a chat log.

Feature

Make yourself part of your machine! With Dropballs you have to manualy type "/r" when you want to see incoming message.

Feature

Support of up to five (5) colors for your nicks to easily manage your chatting. Didn't saw that anywhere, right?

Feature

Everything you've ever written is saved in chat history, so you can revisit your messages anytime you want!
